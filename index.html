The Methodology

The AutoLead Method.

01

Lead Comes In

A potential client fills out your form, clicks your ad, or submits an enquiry — from Facebook, Google, Idealista, Zillow, or anywhere else. AutoLead catches it the instant it lands.

⚡ Trigger: 0 seconds
02

Instant Personalised Outreach

Within 60 seconds, the lead receives a personalised SMS and email using their name and what they enquired about. Your team gets an internal alert too. Nobody falls through the cracks.

🚀 Response: Under 60 seconds
03

Smart Follow-Up Sequence

If they don't reply, the system automatically follows up at 10 minutes, 1 hour, 24 hours, and 72 hours — each message using a different psychological lever: value, urgency, reciprocity, loss aversion.

🔁 Sequence: Auto-pilot
04

AI Qualification

When a lead replies, an AI chatbot qualifies them with 5 key questions — budget, timeline, requirements — so your team only speaks to pre-qualified prospects. No more time wasted on tyre-kickers.

🤖 AI: 24/7 active
05

Automatic Booking

Qualified leads get a calendar link sent automatically and can book a call or viewing at their convenience — without anyone on your team lifting a finger. Deals land in your calendar, ready to close.

📅 Booking: Automatic
06

Long-Term Nurture

Leads that don't convert immediately enter a long-term nurture sequence that keeps your brand top-of-mind. When they're ready to buy, you're the first name they think of. The pipeline compounds.

💡 Nurture: Ongoing
